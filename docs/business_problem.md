# Business Problem & Requirements

## Project Context

Wholesale banking leaders need a reliable weekly view of sales pipeline health, forecast confidence, stale opportunities, and relationship-manager follow-up priorities.

This project simulates an executive Tableau dashboard for weekly business reviews across sales, finance, product, and analytics teams.

## Business Problem

Pipeline reporting is often spread across CRM exports, manual trackers, finance targets, and leadership summaries. This can create inconsistent KPI definitions, limited visibility into stale opportunities, and extra manual effort during weekly reviews.

The goal is to create a single executive-ready view of pipeline performance, forecast risk, and follow-up priorities.

## Primary Users

- Head of Wholesale Banking
- Sales Leadership and Enablement Team
- Finance Business Partners
- Relationship Managers
- Product Leaders
- BI / Analytics Team

## Key Business Questions

- Are we on track to meet quarterly revenue targets?
- Which industries or product lines are driving pipeline growth?
- Which relationship managers have stale or aging opportunities?
- Which late-stage deals are at risk before quarter-end?
- Which clients, industries, or segments should be prioritized?
- Can leadership trust the reported pipeline numbers?

## Dashboard Scope

The dashboard will cover:

- Open pipeline monitoring
- Weighted forecast calculation
- Stale opportunity tracking
- Product, industry, region, and relationship-manager views
- Quarterly target comparison
- Opportunity-level drilldown
- KPI definitions and data-quality rules

## Success Criteria

The dashboard is successful if users can:

- Understand pipeline health within 30 seconds
- Compare weighted forecast against quarterly targets
- Identify stale pipeline by relationship manager, product, industry, and region
- Drill from executive KPIs into opportunity-level detail
- Use consistent KPI definitions across business teams
- Trust the dashboard through documented data-quality checks
